Web Development:

Web development is the process of creating and maintaining websites and web applications. It involves a range of tasks, including coding, scripting, and programming, to bring a web project to life. Web developers use languages like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and various frameworks to build interactive and dynamic websites. They work on both the client-side (front-end) and server-side (back-end) to ensure websites function smoothly and deliver content efficiently. Web development is an ever-evolving field, with constant technological advancements and trends, making it essential for developers to stay updated to create secure, responsive, and user-friendly web experiences. It plays a vital role in the digital world, enabling businesses and individuals to showcase their products, services, and ideas to a global audience.
