SEO (Search Engine Optimization) is making our website more attractive to search engines so that these search engines can show our website to more people when they search for something.

The importance of SEO:
1. When our website shows up early in search results, more people visit it and know about the business.
2. People trust search engines so if our website shows on results it can help to gain trust of users.
3. It makes our website easier to find.
4. We can reach the people all over the world with good SEO.
5. SEO helps our website to grow steadily over time.
