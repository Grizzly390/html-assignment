Favicons are tiny images or icons or logos that represents a website. When we visit a website, we often see a small icon next to website's name in the browser's tab or bookmarks. That little icon is favicon.

Favicons serves two main purpose:
1. They help us quickly identify and recognize a website when we have multiple tabs open in our browser or when we are looking through our bookmarks.

2. Many websites use their logos or brand symbols as favicons, which reinforce their branding and make websites more memorable.
